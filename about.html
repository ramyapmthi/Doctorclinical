Healthcare Support Programs

We are committed to making quality diagnostics accessible for everyone through leading health schemes and dedicated support.

Y.S.R Trust (Arogyasri Scheme)

The diagnostic center provides diagnostic services under the Y.S.R Arogyasri Health Scheme, supporting patients with access to essential medical testing.

EHS Insurance via TPA

The center supports Employee Health Scheme (EHS) and processes insurance claims through TPA partners for eligible patients.

24/7 Help Support

Patients can receive assistance and support for test inquiries, reports, and service guidance round the clock.
