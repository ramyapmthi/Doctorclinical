Education & Awareness

Why Diagnostic Tests Are Important

Understanding your health starts with accurate data. Here is how different tests help in maintaining your well-being.

Blood Tests

Blood tests help doctors evaluate organ function, detect infections, monitor chronic diseases, and identify health conditions early.

X-Ray Imaging

X-rays are essential diagnostic tools that allow doctors to identify bone injuries, lung conditions, and internal abnormalities.

Thyroid Tests

Thyroid testing helps evaluate hormone balance and detect conditions affecting metabolism and energy levels.

Service Details & Instructions

Expand the options below to learn more about the specific tests included and preparation guidelines.

Our blood testing covers numerous diagnostic necessities, from routine evaluations to hormonal profiles.

  • Test Types: CBC, Lipid Profile, Liver Function, Kidney Function, Thyroid.
  • Instructions: Fasting for 10-12 hours is strictly required before a Lipid or fasting blood glucose test. Drinking water is allowed.

Comprehensive examinations using bodily fluids for infection screening and metabolic assessment.

  • Test Types: Urine Routine, CUE, Stool Test, Serology.
  • Instructions: Please collect the first morning sample using the sterile container provided by the clinic for best accuracy.

We leverage advanced digital X-Ray machines to minimize radiation exposure while maximizing image clarity.

  • Test Types: Chest X-Ray, Bone and Joint, Spine Imaging, Abdomen X-Ray.
  • Instructions: Avoid wearing clothes with metallic buttons or zippers. Jewelry must be removed from the area being scanned. Inform the technician if you might be pregnant.

Preventive packages to monitor your long-term health trajectory.

  • Test Types: Basic Metabolic Panel, Vitals Assessment.
  • Instructions: Arrive on an empty stomach. Bring your previous medical records if available to establish a health baseline.
